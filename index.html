SurePa is a scheduling service for people who take medicines and forget to take and which medicine to take. The service will send notifications to the patients when it is time to take the medicines and show them the pillbox's image. SurePa will help patients find the correct pillbox by using an image identifier. The caregiver account could be linked to the patient's account to monitor his treatment process. In this process, the caregiver will be notified whether the patient took the medicines or not.
